How It Works

1. The Chroma-Web Fabric

The umbrella's canopy is constructed from a custom, durable, waterproof fabric embedded with a network of micro-LED fibers. This "Chroma-Web" is what allows for the rich, vibrant color changes without compromising on weather protection.

2. The Smart Handle

The ergonomic handle houses the umbrella's brain: a small, rechargeable battery and a low-power Bluetooth chip. This is where the magic happens, converting your commands into light patterns.

3. App Control & Customization

Connect your Chromabreeze to our dedicated mobile app. Use the intuitive interface to select patterns, customize colors, and set alerts by linking your umbrella to weather apps or your calendar.

4. Effortless Operation

Simply press the button on the handle to power it on and switch between your favorite preset modes instantly. The intelligent system manages power efficiently, giving you hours of brilliant light on a single charge.
